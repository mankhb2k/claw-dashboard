grant trademark rights.

## 2. What the Apache 2.0 license allows

Under the Apache License 2.0 you may:

- Use, modify, and redistribute the **source code**
- Self-host the software for yourself or your organization
- Build derivative products from the code
- Mention that your product is "based on AucoBot" or "powered by AucoBot" in
  **factual, non-misleading** descriptions

The license does **not** allow you to imply endorsement, affiliation, or
official status without permission.

---

## 3. Permitted uses (no written approval required)

You may use AucoBot marks without prior approval when **all** of the following
apply:

1. **Accurate attribution** — e.g. "Built with AucoBot" or "Fork of AucoBot"
2. **No official implication** — you do not use phrases such as:
   - "Official AucoBot"
   - "AucoBot Certified"
   - "AucoBot Enterprise" (unless we grant that separately)
   - "AucoBot Cloud" for a non-official hosted service
3. **No logo modification** — do not alter colors, proportions, or combine the
   logo with other marks in a way that suggests a joint product
4. **Self-hosted / internal use** — running the OSS stack for your own team or
   customers, as long as you do not market it as the official AucoBot Cloud

Reasonable use in documentation, blog posts, conference talks, and issue
discussions is welcome.

Contact **[mankhb2k@gmail.com](mailto:mankhb2k@gmail.com)** before:

| Use case | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Offering a **public hosted service** named or branded AucoBot | Avoid confusion with AucoBot Cloud |

| Using AucoBot **logo on marketing pages**, app stores, or paid products | Brand control |

## 5. Prohibited uses

You must **not**:

- Use "AucoBot" or confusingly similar names (e.g. "AucoBot Pro Hosting",
  "AucoBotX") for a competing hosted platform without permission
- Use our logo as your app icon, favicon, or primary product identity for a
  fork or derivative
- Misrepresent a fork, repackage, or white-label deployment as **AucoBot Cloud**
  or an official release
- Register trademarks or domain names containing "aucobot" in bad faith
- Remove or obscure license and attribution notices in distributed copies

---

## 6. Forks and derivatives

If you fork or redistribute modified code:

- **Rename** your public-facing product if it is substantially different or
  commercially hosted (e.g. "Acme Agent Platform, based on AucoBot")
- Keep copyright and license notices intact
- Do not use the AucoBot logo as your product logo; create your own branding
- A link back to the upstream project (`https://github.com/aucobot/aucobot`) is
  appreciated

## 7. AucoBot Cloud (official hosted service)

**AucoBot Cloud** refers only to the managed service operated by **Trieu Van
Manh** and **The AucoBot Authors**.

Self-hosted deployments, third-party hosts, and community mirrors are **not**
AucoBot Cloud unless we explicitly authorize them in writing. For the current
official service URL, contact [mankhb2k@gmail.com](mailto:mankhb2k@gmail.com).
