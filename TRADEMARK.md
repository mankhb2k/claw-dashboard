- Mention that your product is "based on Claw Dashboard" or "powered by Claw Dashboard" in

official status without permission.

## 3. Permitted uses (no written approval required)

You may use Claw Dashboard marks without prior approval when **all** of the following
apply:

1. **Accurate attribution** — e.g. "Built with Claw Dashboard" or "Fork of Claw Dashboard"
2. **No official implication** — you do not use phrases such as:
   - "Official Claw Dashboard"
   - "Claw Dashboard Certified"
   - "Claw Dashboard Enterprise" (unless we grant that separately)
   - "Claw Dashboard Cloud" for a non-official hosted service
3. **No logo modification** — do not alter colors, proportions, or combine the
   logo with other marks in a way that suggests a joint product
4. **Self-hosted / internal use** — running the OSS stack for your own team or
   customers, as long as you do not market it as the official Claw Dashboard Cloud

Reasonable use in documentation, blog posts, conference talks, and issue
discussions is welcome.

## 5. Prohibited uses

You must **not**:

- Use "Claw Dashboard" or confusingly similar names (e.g. "Claw Dashboard Pro Hosting",
  "Claw DashboardX") for a competing hosted platform without permission
- Use our logo as your app icon, favicon, or primary product identity for a
  fork or derivative
- Misrepresent a fork, repackage, or white-label deployment as **Claw Dashboard Cloud**
  or an official release
- Register trademarks or domain names containing "claw-dashboard" in bad faith
- Remove or obscure license and attribution notices in distributed copies

---

## 6. Forks and derivatives

If you fork or redistribute modified code:

- **Rename** your public-facing product if it is substantially different or
  commercially hosted (e.g. "Acme Agent Platform, based on Claw Dashboard")
- Keep copyright and license notices intact
- Do not use the Claw Dashboard logo as your product logo; create your own branding
- A link back to the upstream project (`https://github.com/openclaw/studio`) is
  appreciated
